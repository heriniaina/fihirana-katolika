Ray ô mahatalanjona
Endrey, ny asanao ry Ray
Mahate-hibanjina ry Ray
Ny soa vitanao.
1- Isaorako Anao, izao tontolo izao
Fa harintananao, asanao, ry Ray
Isaorako Anao, ny olombelona
Fa aina avy aminao, asanao, ry Ray.
2- Isaorako Anao, voaavotra e zahay
Fa irakao, ry Ray, ‘lay zanakao ho anay
Isaorako Anao namindra fo taminay
E, sambatra izahay, asanao, ry Ray.
3- Isaorako Anao, ‘zay nampiray anay
Fa irakao ry Ray, ilay Fanahy ho anay
Isaorako ny Ray, ‘zay nampiray anay
Dera anie ho Anao, anio, mandrakizay
