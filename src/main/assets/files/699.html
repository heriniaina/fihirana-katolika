• Fihirana Hasina p. 814

1-  Be ny ota ka tsy mendrika, malahelo ka manenina
     Nanantena tsy ho tola, kanjo resy ka mivalo

2- Nahatsiaro ho mahery, tsy nijery ny madinika
     Rehareha sy avonavona, kanjo resy ka mivalo

3-  Tia tena ka mijoro, mafy orina sahisahy
      Nanambany nanamavo, kanjo resy ka mivalo

Mifona, mifona fa tsy mendrika e
Fa mpamadika
