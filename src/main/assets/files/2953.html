1-Ndreto ô zahay zagnahary e, Avy magnatogno ô

Entanim-pitiavagna te-hitraotro aminao ô

Tatahôvinay ny vokatry ny tany

Fognôsigny an-tagnan-droa, dignitry angôvonay tô ie

Mahiagna sojabenay, mahiagna sojabenay (in-2)

 

Tatôbe kony ai! Tsy laitry!

Tsisy sahy Azy! Marigny e e!

 

2-Mahavita fahagagagna goailahy Izy mahiagna

Tsy talaky ny fahefagnanazy, mahiagna

Mofo tsy mahafeno helitry, mahiagna

Hiova vatagna Zokibe zi niany ai

Divaigny tapaka vambahy

Hiova lion’zokibe zi niany ai

Igny lahy ai, voatsirambin’ny tagnagna (in-2)

 

3-Ndreto ô, zahay zagnahary e, vory aketo niany

No indro hitôraka vato masigny e ia

Ankasitraho ny hasigny indesinay

Valim-pitiavanao zahay fagnomezan’ny fonay jiaby e e
