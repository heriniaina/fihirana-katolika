• Fihirana Vaovao p. 165

1. Tonga ity 'lay andro soa 'zay nandrasako hatrizay:
Falifaly aho, ry Jesoa, fa notiavin'izy Ray.

Sambatra aho, fa nandray anao, ry Jesoa Tompo ô;
Jesoa ô, vonona ho tia anao ity zandry malalanao.

2. Hafaliana sesilany no atolotrao, Kristy:
Ka hiravo hatrany hatrany 'reo mandray ny Eokaristia.
