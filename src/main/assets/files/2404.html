• Antsao ny Tompo p. 289

(Laurence)

Zaho mino ô ! ‘zaho mino ô !
‘Zaho mino lahaby, miravoravo ô !

‘Zaho mino ô! Zañahary.

Ray mahefa, be fitiavana,
Mpahary ny lanitra sy ny tany e à !
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
Zañahary nanao ny raha jiaby,
Na hita maso na tsy hita maso ià !
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
Mino zaho ô ! ‘zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô! (in-2).

‘Zaho mino an’i Jesoa Tompo,
Naterak’i Maria Virjiny e !
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
Nijaly sy maty ho antsika jiaby,
Nitsangan-ko velona an-kafaliana,
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
Mino zaho ô ! ‘zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !(in-2).

‘Zaho mino ny Fanahy Masina,
Loharano nipoiran’ny aina e !
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
Mpankahery sy Mpanamasina,
Mendrik’haja sy voninahitra,
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
Mino zaho ô ! ‘zaho mino, ô Zañahariko ô !(in-2).

‘Zaho mino ny Fiangonana Masina,
‘Zay manerana ny tany jiaby e !
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
‘Zaho mino ny Batemy tôkana,
‘Zay manala ny fahotana,
‘Zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô !
Mino zaho ô ! ‘zaho mino ô ! Zañahariko ô ! (in-2).
