Mifankatiava e, mifankatiava,
Mifankatiava e, toy ny nitiavako anareo.
 
1 Ny Zoky iombonana anie no nilaza,
 Ka mifankatiava e,
 Fitiavana iray no nitiavany antsika,
 Ka mivankatiava e.
 
2 Ny fitiavany raha mitoetra amintsika...
 Hataony tokoa 'zay rehetra irintsika...
 
3 Iray ny Fanahy nananganana antsika...
 Mpandova ny Ray, samy zanany isika...
