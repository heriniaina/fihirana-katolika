• Fihirana Hasina p. 823

O Jesoa, ô Jesoa, lehilahy mahery e!
Miaiky anao ô, miaiky e herinao iahy e!
Tohano hahasahy e! e! e!
Hijoboky ame lalilaly e! e! e!

Mipitsa anay andra Jesoa
Gne dina mifehy  ame fomban-draza
Mamoritra anay, ts'hanagna hery
Gne dina laha ame tahotry vaha gn'hegna maso
E Fagnahinao mahery omeo anay e!
Hahasahianay hisetry amy laly (3)

Magnabotry anay andra Jesoa
Gne oha-drohogno'olo "Hava laha manamaha"
Mamoritry enay ts'hahafaha mifofotry
Mamelona lava gne fihavana
E Fagnahinao fitiava omeo anay e!
Hifampitagnanay hisetry amy laly (3)

Tamba manaka hivavaky rô Jesoa
Gne (Gne) fahasahiragna mamely enay
Mitotototo avao e fagnahy e
E Fagnahinao tsara omeo anay e!
Hifamonjenay zandrinao ame laly (3)
