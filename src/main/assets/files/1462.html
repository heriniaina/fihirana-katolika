Hankany an-danitra aho
Hahita an'i Maria,
Hankany an-danitra aho
'Zay paradisanay.
 
1 Hankany indray andro any
 Hahita an'ilay Reny,
 Hijery tanin-dRay
 'Zay lovanay tokoa.
 
2 Hankany indray andro any
 Handray ny lovanay,
 Hivolon-tompony ao
 'Zahay mandrakizay.
 
3 Hankany indray andro any
 Finoana mafy tsara
 Sy fitiavam-be
 No tohatra mankany.
 
4 Hankany indray andro any
 Hampiato alahelo,
 Hamafa ranomaso,
 Hanovo haravoana.
 
5 Hankany indray andro any
 Ho lavitra ny tany,
 Hitoetra akaikinao,
 Ho soa mandrakizay.
 
6 Hankany indray andro any
 Ny zanakao, ry Neny,
 Hamantan-trano soa
 Anilanao tokoa.
