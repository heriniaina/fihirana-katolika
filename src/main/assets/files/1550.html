Mitiava mariñy, (indroa)
Ataovy izay tianao hatao;
Fitiavana e e ia, mitiava mariñy:
Mahay mifampizara, mahay mandanjalanja,
Tsy manam-pahataperana, misy fandeferana;
Fitiavana e e ia, mitiava mariñy. (indroa)
