Aleloia ee ia ! Masigny tenin-Janahary ie, ia
Raisinay an-kafaliana ee ia
O ia ee ia, Raisinay an-kafaliana ee, ia.

Masigny tenin-Janahary ie, ia.
Raisinay an-kafaliana ee, ia

  1. Tenin-Janahary masigny an-tany e ia.
    Ankatoavinay e ia
    Loharanon’ainy tsy mety ritra.
    Hanovozana herim-pitiavana
         Masigny tenin-Janahary ie, ia.
         Raisinay an-kafaliana ee, ia.
     
  2. Tenin-Janahary tsy lany tsiro ô ia
    Sakafom-pahay ankatoavinay e ia
    Loharanon’ainy tsy mety ritra
    Hanovozona herim-pitiavana
        Masigny tenin-Janahary ie, ia.
        Raisinay an-kafaliana ee, ia.
     
  3. Tenin-Janahary mampiray antsika e ia.
    Maniraka hifankatia e ia
    Mampifaly ny malahelo ô ia.
    Fiarovan-tena amin’ny ady
    Mandresy lahatra ireo tsy tia e ia.
        Masigny tenin-Janahary ie, ia.
        Raisinay an-kafaliana ee, ia.
