La vareake Ndranañahare

Ty hasaverena’ao lako toy,

Namotorana’ao ty soa tsy hay volañe

Raha manañ’ay, saina fo fanahy,

Mahavelo rañandria

Laha manan-tsifa le nirakaraka,

Laha manan-tsafo le nidoladola

Laha nanañ’ohy le nipiopioke,

Mahavelo, misaotse Baba.

1 – Niasa anay Iha, namboatse kila raha

Nanao ze hahasambatse anay e, e !

Nampisy nytsy misy, nanao ny zava-boary,

Mba hiadam-pianaritsy reto zanakao.

2 – Nalefa’ao bakao, Jesoa Anaki’ao

Tsy roa tsy telo fe namea avao

Vinono le naito, natsanga’ao avao

Satria teanao hitohy ndraike ñy filongoa.

 

3 – Tsy mimpoly hatea lahy Ndranañahare,

Tsy raha lisatse ro natao antsika,

Haaveloma bevata tsy tetoy nindraike

Misaotse bebe mahavelo lahy e.
