Noely, Noely, voninahitra
Noely, Noely, ho an’Andriamanitra
Noely, Noely, voninahitra ho Azy
Noely, Noely, any an-tampon’ny lanitra
 
            Fa misy zaza teraka ho antsika
            Mpanjaka Izy, zanaky ny avo indrindra
            Tao Betlehema no tonga teto an-tany,
            Dia ilay Jesoa terak’i Maria. (indroa)
